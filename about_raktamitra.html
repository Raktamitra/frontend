About RaktaMitra

Who We Are

RaktaMitra is a youth-led initiative focused on saving lives through efficient, compassionate blood donation services. We're powered by volunteers and supported by communities across India.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients using technology, awareness, and a human-first approach. We aim to ensure that no life is lost due to the unavailability of blood.

Why We Exist

  • Thousands in India struggle to find blood on time each year.
  • Lack of awareness and real-time coordination costs lives.
  • We solve this via fast matching, verified donors, and mobilization.

What We Do

  • Host donation drives across cities
  • Connect patients to nearby eligible donors
  • Partner with hospitals & verified blood banks
  • Educate people about safe donation
  • Build a responsive volunteer network

Our Impact So Far

  • 5000+ registered donors
  • 100+ emergency matches made
  • Presence in 8+ cities
  • 150+ lives saved directly through our work

Our Vision

A future where blood donation is fast, stigma-free, and fully supported. We want to ensure no patient suffers due to lack of access to safe blood.
