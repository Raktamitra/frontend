About Ashayam

Ashayam is a youth-driven movement dedicated to community service and creating positive change in society. It focuses on health, education, the environment, and social awareness. Volunteers across India organize blood donation drives, health camps, awareness events, and educational support for underprivileged communities.

Our Journey

Ashayam Foundation (Reg. No: 12/2023) began in Tirupati with just three passionate volunteers and a dream — to make the world a better place. What started small has now grown into a vibrant network with eight active units in Tirupati, Kakinada, Guntur, Nellore, Vizag, Hyderabad, Bengaluru, and Chennai.

Ashayam is more than just an organization; it's a family of young changemakers aged 18 to 27, all united by compassion and a desire to serve. It's a movement to build a kinder, safer, more equal world for everyone.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To build a society where every individual has access to basic needs, dignity, and opportunity — regardless of background or status.

Mission: To empower youth to become proactive contributors to community welfare through volunteerism, social awareness, and compassionate action.

Core Values

Empathy

We listen, understand, and support the people we serve with genuine compassion.

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our initiatives.

Youth Leadership

We believe in the power of young minds to lead change and inspire others.

Flagship Initiatives

  • RaktaMitra: A pan-India blood donation support system and helpline.
  • Vidya: Free academic mentorship and tuition for underprivileged students.
  • Haritha: Tree plantation and green zone preservation drives.
  • Arogya Camps: Free health check-up and awareness camps in rural areas.
