Blood Type Compatibility Checker

Compatible Donor Blood Groups

Compatibility Rules:

  • Group O can donate to all groups (universal donor)
  • Group A can donate to A and AB
  • Group B can donate to B and AB
  • Group AB can donate only to AB
  • Rh-negative can donate to Rh-positive and Rh-negative
  • Rh-positive can donate only to Rh-positive
