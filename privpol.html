Privacy Policy for RaktaMitra

Effective Date: 26th July 2025

At RaktaMitra, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our blood donation app.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Blood Type
  • Email Address / Phone Number
  • Gender
  • Address and Location (for matching donors and recipients nearby)

Health-Related Information:

  • Last donation date
  • Medical history relevant to blood donation
  • Eligibility status

Device and Usage Data:

  • Device ID and operating system
  • IP address
  • App usage data (e.g., pages viewed, features used)

How We Use Your Information

  • Match donors with recipients
  • Notify users about nearby donation needs
  • Maintain medical safety protocols
  • Improve our services
  • Send updates, alerts, and donation reminders
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your data with:

  • Certified blood banks and hospitals (only with your consent)
  • Emergency responders (in urgent need scenarios)
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., cloud storage or analytics) under strict confidentiality
  • Authorities if legally required

Data Security

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL)
  • Secure user authentication
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits

Your Rights

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Correct or update your information
  • Delete your account and data permanently
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Location Data

We use your location to show nearby donation requests and blood banks. You can disable location permissions in your device settings, but this may limit some functionality.

Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar tools for analytics and performance monitoring. These tools help improve user experience but do not collect personally identifiable information.
